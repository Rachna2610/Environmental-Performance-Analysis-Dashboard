# KPI Definitions

## AQI
Air Quality Index

## PM2.5
Fine particulate matter concentration.

## PM10
Coarse particulate matter concentration.

## NO2
Nitrogen dioxide concentration.

## SO2
Sulfur dioxide concentration.

## CO
Carbon monoxide concentration.

## Ozone
Ground-level ozone concentration.

## Dashboard KPIs

1. Average AQI
2. Maximum AQI
3. Average PM2.5
4. Average PM10
5. Average NO2
6. Average SO2
7. Average CO
8. Average Ozone
