# Dataset Understanding

## Dataset Name
Delhi Air Quality Dataset

## Columns

1. Date - Day of observation
2. Month - Month of observation
3. Year - Year of observation
4. Holidays_Days - Indicates holiday status
5. PM2.5 - Fine particulate matter concentration
6. PM10 - Coarse particulate matter concentration
7. NO2 - Nitrogen dioxide concentration
8. SO2 - Sulfur dioxide concentration
9. CO - Carbon monoxide concentration
10. Ozone - Ozone concentration
11. AQI - Air Quality Index

## Target KPI
AQI (Air Quality Index)

## Secondary KPIs
- PM2.5
- PM10
- NO2
- SO2
- CO
- Ozone
